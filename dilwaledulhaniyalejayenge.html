Movie-SYNOPSIS/PLOT:

When Raj meets Simran in Europe, it isn’t love at first sight but when Simran moves to India for an arranged marriage, love makes its presence felt.Of Indian-origin Chaudhry Baldev Singh is settled in London, England with his family consisting of his wife Lajwanti ‘Lajjo’ and 2 daughters Rajeshwari alias ‘Chukti’, his eldest daughter being Simran. When Simran was much younger Baldev had set her marriage with his childhood friend’s son Ajit back in India; but when Simran goes on a trip to Europe she meets with Raj Malhotra and both fall in love. When she confides to her dad he is enraged about this and immediately takes Simran to India – where she must undergo preparations for her marriage to Ajit.
