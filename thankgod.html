Movie-SYNOPSIS/PLOT:

The Film Is A Remake Of Anders Matthesen’s 2009 Norwegian Film “Sorte kugler”{aka ‘What Goes Around’}. The Story Revolves Around An Egoistic Real Estate Broker In Huge Debts, Who Meets With An Accident. As He Gains Consciousness, He Realises That He Is In Heaven. Chitragupta Appears In Front Of Him And Informs Him That He Will Have To Play A “Game Of Life”. If He Manages To Win, He Will Be Sent Back To Earth And If He Loses, He Will Be Sent To Hell !!
